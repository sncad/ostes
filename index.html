About Choco Deli


Tradition since 2012

Choco Deli was founded in 2012 by Sonata in Jakarta, Indonesia. Choco Deli sells freshly baked cakes of all kinds, ready to cater to all your needs and cravings. We make beautiful cakes for special events such as birthdays, celebrations, weddings, parties, or a customized present for your loved ones! We bake each of our cake creations with love and can be adjusted to your to your special needs. Choco Deli is the best place to go to order a delicious and beautiful cakes. Here we offer you some of the best selling cakes in our store! We only use the finest materials.

All of our cakes are vegan. We do not use any animal products, eggs, or dairy in our ingredients. Our cakes are freshly made everyday straight from the oven and 100% organic, healthy, and a true delicacy for everyone who craves for sweetness.
